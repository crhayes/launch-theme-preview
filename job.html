Software Engineering Manager, Growth

Who we are

About Launch

We’re not your average finance crowd; we’re a vibrant fintech startup here to shake things up. Our mission? Transforming financial services with cutting‑edge tech and a dash of excitement. Whether you’re a coding genius, finance pro, or a creative problem-solver, there’s a spot for you in our diverse team. Join us in rewriting the rules of finance and let’s create something extraordinary together. Explore our open roles and be part of the fintech revolution!

About the team

Welcome to our Growth Engineering Team, where ideas take flight and innovation drives our expansion! As a part of this dynamic crew, you’ll play a key role in transforming concepts into impactful solutions that propel our company forward. We’re all about crafting inventive strategies and developing cutting‑edge features. If you’re passionate about shaping our company’s future, love collaboration, and thrive on growth challenges, join us in building something extraordinary!

What you’ll do

You’ll lead a talented group of engineers in the pursuit of innovation and expansion. Your role involves shaping the technical vision, driving strategy, and overseeing the implementation of growth-oriented projects. From optimizing user experiences to deploying cutting‑edge features, you’ll play a pivotal role in steering our company towards new heights. If you’re a dynamic leader passionate about combining technology with strategic growth, join us in redefining what’s possible in the world of software engineering. Your impact awaits!

Responsibilities

  • Lead Team Innovation: Inspire and lead a team of engineers, fostering a collaborative and innovative culture.
  • Define Technical Vision: Shape and communicate the technical vision for growth initiatives, aligning it with company goals.
  • Drive Strategy Execution: Oversee the implementation of growth projects, ensuring alignment with business objectives.
  • Enhance User Experiences: Collaborate on optimizing user experiences, using technical solutions to drive engagement.
  • Promote Continuous Improvement: Foster a culture of ongoing improvement, keeping the team at the forefront of tech trends and best practices.

Who you are

We’re looking for someone who meets the minimum requirements to be considered for the role. If you meet these requirements, you are encouraged to apply. The preferred qualifications are a bonus, not a requirement.

Minimum requirements

  • 2+ years of experience in software development
  • Professional working proficiency in English
  • Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science or related field or equivalent work experience

Preferred qualifications

  • Proven track record of effectively leading and inspiring software engineering teams, driving collaboration, and fostering a culture of innovation.
  • Ability to shape and communicate a clear technical vision for growth initiatives, aligning it with broader company goals and strategies.
  • Strong project management skills with a demonstrated ability to oversee and drive the execution of growth-oriented projects from conception to completion.
  • Solid background in software engineering with a deep understanding of current technologies and best practices, enabling effective decision‑making and guidance for the team.
  • A passion for staying at the forefront of technology trends, coupled with a commitment to fostering a culture of continuous improvement and innovation within the engineering team.

We look forward to hearing from you

If you’re all about driving growth, shaping tech vision, and fostering a collaborative vibe, let’s connect. Your unique skills could be just what we need to take our team to the next level. Ready to dive in? We’re looking forward to it!
